77 /*
78  * NumberOfValues is the number of EVENTS encoded in the various
79  * 3/4 PACK Bytes. Non-multiples of (3) [ for 3-packed ] or (4)
80  * [ for 4-packed ] indicates the trailing events are transmitted
81  * as -0- and should be ignored (and NOT interpreted as an ENDMARK)
82  * A value of -0- indicates there are no events to report (a NULL).
83  *
84  * furthermore, the events are 'vector' operations - the first encoded event
85  * operates on the (FirstEvent) attribute, the second encoded event
86  * operates on the (FirstEvent+1) attribute, and so forth.
