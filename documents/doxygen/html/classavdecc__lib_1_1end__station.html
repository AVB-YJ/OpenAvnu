)
pure virtual

Send a READ_DESCRIPTOR command to read a descriptor from an AVDECC Entity. Reading a descriptor can be performed by any AVDECC Controller even when the AVDECC Entitys locked or acquired as the act of reading the descriptor does not affect the AVDECC Entity state.

매개변수
notification_idA void pointer to the unique identifier associated with the command.
desc_typeThe type of the descriptor to be read by the command.
desc_indexThe index of the descriptor to be read by the command.
